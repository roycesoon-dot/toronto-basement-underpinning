## 2. Offer Suite & Pricing

* **Basement Underpinning & Lowering:** Deep excavation and installation of new concrete footings to extend foundation walls, safely increasing basement ceiling heights to 8 feet or higher.
* **Underpinning Pricing:** Projects typically cost between $80 to $500 per linear foot. A standard basement project ranges from $20,000 to $50,000 depending on depth and complexity.
* **Drawings & Permits:** Coordination with architects to provide proper structural drawings and secure necessary city permits within several weeks before work begins.
* **Inside Waterproofing:** Complete interior waterproofing of all foundation walls to prevent flood and water damage, frequently paired with the underpinning process.
* **Sump Pump & Battery Backups:** Installation of robust sump pumps and backup systems to actively manage basement water accumulation.
* **Basement Finishing:** Comprehensive remodeling after underpinning, creating new layouts and completely finishing the newly lowered space for immediate residential use.
* **Drains, Plumbing, and Concrete:** General underground plumbing, drain maintenance, and foundational concrete repairs to support a dry and structurally sound home.
* **Service Radius:** Greater Toronto Area.
* **Operating Hours:** Standard local business hours for support, with active construction scheduling year round.
